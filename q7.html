ellyse perry

Ellyse Alexandra Perry (born 3 November 1990) is an Australian sportswoman who has represented her country in cricket and soccer. Having debuted for both the national cricket team and the national soccer team at the age of 16, she is the youngest Australian to play international cricket and the first to have appeared in both ICC and FIFA World Cups (Only appeared as a substitute after the 89th minute for the Matildas team).[3] Gradually becoming a single-sport professional athlete from 2014 onward,[4] Perry's acclaimed cricket career has continued to flourish and she is widely regarded to be the greatest woman cricketer of all time.
